Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple recipe is too good to make. you can try it

Ingredients

  • 1/2 sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Six medium size apples, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 Farenhite
  2. In the bowl,mix the bowl and cinnamon
  3. Toss the apple in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
